# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Nacre is a technical project. Participation should be respectful, direct, and focused on improving the software.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful of different experience levels and operating environments.
- Keep discussion on the code, behavior, documentation, and user impact.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about project scope and compatibility boundaries.
- Report security issues privately according to `SECURITY.md`.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Repeated off-topic arguments after a maintainer has closed a discussion.
- Publishing private security reports before a fix or advisory is ready.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent loss of participation privileges.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the project maintainer. Include relevant links, screenshots, or context when possible.
